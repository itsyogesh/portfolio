Aradhana is a religion and spirituality platform — essentially "karaoke for aarti." It's a digital companion for Hindu temple experiences, prayer streaming, and spiritual practice tracking.

## Planned Products

- **Aradhana Suno** — Aarti streaming and karaoke platform across web, mobile, and social channels
- **Aradhana Wallpapers** — Hindu devotional wallpaper collection
- **Akhand Jyoti Tracker** — Prayer and devotion progress tracking app
