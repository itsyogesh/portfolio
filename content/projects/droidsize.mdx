Droidsize Technologies is a product development studio I co-founded with Neeraj Kumar. We build proprietary SaaS and AI products while helping external founders ship complete products from concept to deployment.

## What We Build

Our portfolio spans developer tools, AI products, and consumer applications:

- **Collective Domains** — Domain marketplace and portfolio management
- **Painini** — AI-powered grammar and writing tool
- **Turbocamp** — Full-stack SaaS starter kit
- **TripleWave** — AI document generation combining Canva and Google Docs

## How We Work

We use modern stacks (Next.js, Astro, Firebase) and deploy on Vercel. The team has shipped 7+ live products with 8 more in active development across 14 GitHub repositories.
