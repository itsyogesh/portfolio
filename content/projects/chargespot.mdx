Chargespot is an EV charging platform I built through Charge23 Labs, a DPIIT-recognized startup incubated at AIC Sangam.

## The Stack

This is one of the most technically diverse projects I've worked on:

- **Mobile** — Flutter/Dart apps for iOS and Android
- **Backend** — Python/Django with Unfold Admin
- **Station Server** — Go-based OCPP/OCPI protocol handler for charging hardware
- **Web Dashboard** — TypeScript
- **Infrastructure** — Terraform on AWS

## Standards Compliance

Full support for OCPP v2.0-2.2.1 and OCPI v2.0-2.2.1, the industry standards for EV charging communication.
